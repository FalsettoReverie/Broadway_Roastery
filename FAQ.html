Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you sell coffee beans?
A: We sell coffee beans and fresh coffee ground.
Q: Where does your coffee come from?
A: Although we import coffee from around the world, most of our coffey is imported from Vietnam.
Q: Does your supplier ever change?
A: We have been with the same supplier for many years, and are continually satisfied with the quality of the coffee beans they provide us.
Q: Do you use any artificial flavors, food additives or GMOs?
A: No we do not, our coffee is 100% natural.
Q: Is there a difference between your two locations?
A: We frequently mix and rotate staff between the locations, so it is unlikely you will notice any difference in service.
