Who are we and what do we do

Hi, I'm Simon a Web designer for the Broadway Roastery. You might be wondering how I ended up at this job. Well you see a long time ago I drank my first cup of coffee in the early hours of the morning. It tasted like burnt charcoal and I vowed to never drink a cup of coffee every again. But as time went one my appreciation for a good cup of joe has lead me to pursue in a career that favors both spectrums of what I love to do (Drink coffee and write some great code!) I designed this site to give everyone a chance to fully enjoy a great cup of coffee
